# Design System Inspired by Lensrentals

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Lensrentals embodies a professional, equipment-focused brand identity rooted in trust and precision. The design system balances technical sophistication with accessibility, featuring a deep navy-blue foundation that conveys reliability and expertise in the camera rental industry. Warm accent colors—particularly orange and teal—inject energy and guide user action, while generous whitespace and clean typography support content clarity. The visual language prioritizes functional elegance, supporting both complex product catalogs and streamlined transaction flows. High-contrast text and strategic color cues ensure intuitive navigation across photography professionals and enthusiasts alike.

**Key Characteristics**

- Deep navy-blue primary palette projecting authority and trust
- Teal and orange accent colors for CTAs and highlights
- Clean, functional typography with strong hierarchical contrast
- High-contrast neutral text (`#333333`, `#000000`) for legibility
- Professional, minimal aesthetic avoiding unnecessary decoration
- Warm-to-cool color balance creating visual sophistication
- Accessibility-first approach with sufficient color and text contrast
